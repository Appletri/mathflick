About Math Flick

If you are a fan of first-person shooters like Valorant and Counter-Strike, then you most likely know about aim trainers. These are programs used to help train players use their mouse as quick and prescise as looking at a point on the screen with your eyes. Check out this exmaple of a professional flicking their mouse!

Math Flick combines the quick-paced and precise mouse control with an added element of arithmetic! Give it a try!
